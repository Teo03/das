About MSE

What is MSE?

Founded in 1995, the Macedonian Stock Exchange (MSE) is the principal marketplace for trading securities in North Macedonia. Over the years, MSE has grown to become a vital component of the country's financial system, providing a transparent and regulated environment for investors, companies, and financial institutions.

Mission

The MSE is dedicated to promoting an efficient, transparent, and secure trading environment that supports economic growth and development. By fostering investor confidence and facilitating capital formation, we aim to be a cornerstone of the Macedonian financial landscape.
